San Ardo

Locals like to hang out on the beach of Rancho Rico Creek and wade up the river to swim in deep pools.

Tresspass potential — parts of this area is on private land, be tresspass aware.

Flow rate: 71 cfs — Below recommended flow rate From the Bradley Bridge guage. Best flows are between 80 and 600 CFS. Updated August 26, 2021 at 4:07 pm.

Directions

Driving directions

Park at the end of Jolon Road, where there is an open gate built into a tall wildlife fence. Pass throught the fence and turn left, following the trail to the dry wash of Rancho Rico Creek. Turn right and follow the wash and the old bridge footings to the river, around 300 yards. There is a large beach along the river.
