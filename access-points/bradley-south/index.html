This is the typical put-in for the popular Bradley tubing trip. Note that this is land on Camp Roberts, and whenever there is active training in the area, your access to the river can be closed.

Directions

Park on either end of Bradley road and follow the path to the west of the road towards the river. There is a clear opening in the fence just below the road.
