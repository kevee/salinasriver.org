Blanco Bridge

Best flow
0 to 100 cfs at Chualar
Water level
1750 cfs & 7.95 feet deep Updated March 9, 2024 at 6:31 pm
Driving directions
View in Google Maps
Help protect your river rights. Let us know if you use this access point.

A useful put-in or take-out, but not a very fun place to hang out by the river.

Note that if there is any significant flow, then the river mouth could be breached and you should not attempt going down river.

Directions

Park on the Salinas side of the bridge, on a large pull-out in front of some mail boxes. The trail is on the right as you face the bridge.
