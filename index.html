The Salinas River

A recreation guide

This is a guide for enjoying the Salinas River. It flows for 170 miles south to north. It is home to beavers and bald eagles. Residents noodle on its banks for catfish, and cool off in its silty waters in the summer. Land owners, farmers, and oilmen have hidden it on purpose.
